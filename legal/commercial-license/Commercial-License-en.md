# Commercial Software License Agreement (Template) — EN Version

> **Status**: Draft template to be tailored case-by-case.  
> **Required before publication**: final review and approval by a France-qualified IP/software legal counsel.

## 1. Parties

This agreement is entered into by and between:

- **Licensor**: [Legal name], [corporate form], [registration], [registered office], represented by [name/title].
- **Licensee**: [Legal name], [corporate form], [registration], [registered office], represented by [name/title].

Together the “Parties” and each a “Party”.

## 2. Definitions

- **Software**: [software name], including [modules, APIs, documentation] described in the Annexes.
- **Licensee Products/Services**: products or services integrating or using the Software.
- **Related Revenue**: net revenue (excluding VAT/sales taxes) actually collected by Licensee and directly attributable to exploitation of the Software.
- **Territory**: [France / EU / Worldwide].

## 3. Purpose

Licensor grants Licensee a license to use and commercially exploit the Software under the terms set forth herein.

## 4. Scope of rights granted

Subject to compliance with this Agreement and payment of royalties:

1. **Granted rights** (adjust as needed):
   - internal use,
   - integration into Licensee products/services,
   - technical reproductions required for operation,
   - distribution of Licensee products/services embedding the Software,
   - sublicensing only if expressly authorized.
2. **License type**: [non-exclusive/exclusive], non-transferable, non-sublicensable unless expressly stated.
3. **Restrictions**: no reverse engineering, decompilation, removal of IP notices, or use outside agreed scope/territory, except where mandatory law allows.
4. **Reservation of rights**: all non-expressly granted rights remain with Licensor.

## 5. IP ownership

All intellectual property rights in and to the Software remain solely with Licensor (or its licensors). No ownership transfer occurs.

## 6. Royalties — Revenue share formula

1. **Royalty rate**: Licensee shall pay **[X]% of Related Revenue**.
2. **Calculation base**:
   - included: sales/subscription/service revenue directly linked to Software usage;
   - excluded: VAT/sales taxes, documented rebates, chargebacks, cancellations, finally uncollectible receivables.
3. **Attribution methodology**:
   - primary: analytical allocation by SKU/offer;
   - fallback for bundled offers: objective allocation key defined in `pricing-schedule.md`.

## 7. Minimum guarantee (if applicable)

1. Licensee shall pay an annual **minimum guarantee** of **[amount] EUR**.
2. Variable royalties are credited against the minimum guarantee for the relevant period.
3. Any shortfall remains payable at annual true-up.

## 8. Payment terms

- Reporting and invoicing frequency: [monthly/quarterly].
- Statement and invoice deadline: no later than day [15] after period-end.
- Payment due: [30] days end-of-month (or [30] days from invoice date), via bank transfer.
- Currency: EUR unless otherwise stated.

## 9. Reporting obligations

Each reporting period, Licensee provides at least:

- gross and net Related Revenue,
- calculation assumptions,
- breakdown by product/service/territory,
- relevant license/user/transaction metrics,
- refunds/credits/bad debt,
- reasonably requested supporting accounting records.

Records must be retained for [5] years.

## 10. Audit right

Licensor may appoint an independent auditor (subject to confidentiality) to audit Licensee's books related to this Agreement up to [once] per year (except in case of serious discrepancy), with at least [15] business days' prior notice.

If underpayment exceeds [5]%, Licensee bears reasonable audit costs and promptly pays all outstanding amounts.

## 11. Late payment and penalties

Late payments accrue default interest at the applicable legal rate plus [10] percentage points (or compliant contractual rate), plus any statutory recovery indemnity where applicable.

Licensor may suspend license rights after formal notice remains uncured for [15] days.

## 12. Termination and survival

- Initial term: [12/24/36] months, auto-renewing for [12]-month periods unless notice of non-renewal is given [3] months before term end.
- Termination for material breach if uncured within [30] days after notice.
- Effects: cease use/distribution (subject to agreed sell-off), settle outstanding sums.
- Surviving clauses: IP ownership, payment obligations, audit, confidentiality, liability regime, governing law and jurisdiction.

## 13. Governing law and jurisdiction

This Agreement is governed by **French law**.

Any dispute shall be submitted to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of **[Paris, France]**, subject to mandatory legal provisions.
